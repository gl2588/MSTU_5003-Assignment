Mewtwo

Pokémon Type: Psychic
Weakness: Bug, Dark, Ghost
Evotions: This Pokémon does not evolve.

Mewtwo is a Pokémon that was created by genetic manipulation. However, even though the scientific power of humans created this Pokémon's body, they failed to endow Mewtwo with a compassionate heart.

Training

EV yield: 3 Special Attack
Catch rate: 3 (0.4% with PokéBall, full HP)
Base Friendship: 0 (lower than normal)
Growth Rate: Slow

Mewtwo Changes
In Generation 1, Mewtwo has a base Special stat of 154.
In Generations 1-4, Mewtwo has a base experience yield of 220.
In Generation 2, Mewtwo has a base Friendship value of 70.
